Company Vacation Policy

All employees are entitled to 15 vacation days per year.
Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.25 days per month.
Employees must submit vacation requests at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

Health Insurance

The company offers comprehensive health insurance through Blue Cross.
Dental and vision insurance are available as optional add-ons.
New employees have 30 days to enroll in health insurance plans.
